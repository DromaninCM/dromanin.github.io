Research Interests

My main scientific interest is the theoretical modelling of condensed matter, with emphasis on the connection to experimental measurements. In particular I am interested in the ab-initio investigations of electron-phonon interactions, with a focus on superconductivity and charge density waves in low dimensional systems. Recently, I became also interested in optical properties of materials and tensor network theory.
